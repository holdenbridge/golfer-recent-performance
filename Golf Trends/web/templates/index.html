About

What is this?

The idea of momentum is highly debated in the sports analytics community. Most analytically driven models that power sportsbooks like FanDuel and DraftKings are centered around a player's "true talent" and often place little to no emphasis on if a player has been hot or cold recently. My hypothesis is that given how much of a mental battle golf is, momentum can have a real impact on a player's performance.
